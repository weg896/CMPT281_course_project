Drug testing completed

The recent round of drug testing has been completed and while it has been found that none of the bears is using performance enhancing drugs there have been some serious questions raised. One of the main concerns is that Puffy appears to have no blood whatsoever. Either the lab technicians are incompetent or somethingg strange is afoot. The drug testing for Safety bear was compromised as well when it was found that the blood sample given was human blood. We think it likely that it is blood from someone he mauled during the recent peta demonstration.

Peta at it again

Activist group Peta have attempted once again to free one of our bears from captivity the bear they released was Safety bear. As soon as safety bear was free from his cage he began attacking the activists mauling several before being tranquilized. 5 protesteers were injured. William Selby a huge fan of the sport told reporters "what did they expect releasing a trained killer"
